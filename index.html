Background

My technical background includes ~20 years of technology and technology management experience. Technical certifications: SANS GSEC, GCIH, GCIA, ISC(2) CISSP, Sec+, etc. Coding experience: Python, PHP, Perl, Java, C, very limited Assembly etc. Currently pursuing a graduate studies and teaching opportunities.

I am have been an adjunct faculty at Northern Virginia Community College IET department, teaching Cybersecurity courses, usually ITP270 - Programming for Cybersecurity and at George Mason University in the ISOM (Information Systems & Operations Management) Department.

Research Interests

  • Human Factors in Cybersecurity
  • Privacy Enhancing Technologies
  • Endpoint/Mobile Device Security
