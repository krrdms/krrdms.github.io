Background

My technical background includes ~20 years of technology and technology management experience. Technical certifications: SANS GPEN, GCIA, GCIH, GSEC, ISC(2) CISSP, Sec+, etc. Coding experience: Python, PHP, Perl, Java, C, limited x86 Assembly etc. Recently completed doctoral study.

I have been an adjunct faculty at Northern Virginia Community College IET department, teaching cybersecurity courses, usually ITP270 - Programming for Cybersecurity and at George Mason University in the ISOM (Information Systems & Operations Management) Department, MIS320.
